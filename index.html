BIRDMINTON

Rules Of The Game

    Power Badminton is an awesome side-scrolling badminton game in which you must show off your skills with a racket and shuttlecock. You must utilize a range of different shots and smashes and try to win the game. You can choose from various character options.There is an also power up that you can use to make your shots more powerful. Show of your power badminton skills today!

    ← (Move Left)
