BIRDMINTON

Rules Of The Game

  • Multiplayer game, choose from either birdy or piggy. There are huge varieties present in the game
  • 3 different types of Shots including clear (back to back), drop (front to front) and intermediate
  • Each player has total of 5 eggs, you lose once all of your eggs are broken
  • Faults include Net, outside of the playground but you dont lose your eggs with these faults
  • You lose your egg if the shuttle lands on your side of the court
  • You can jump and move anywhere in the court

CONTROLS

Left Player

    W (Jump)
    A (Move Left)
    D (Move Right)
    S (Do Service)
    Q (Hit Long with Jump )
    E (Hit Short)

Right Player

    ↑ (Jump)
    ← (Move Left)
    →(Move Right)
    4 (Hit Long with Jump )
    5 (Hit Short)
